Hi, I'm Hannah Kang!

I am a Junior at Carnegie Mellon University pursuing both Fine Arts and Human Computer Interaction with a minor in Design. This past summer I joined 3M as a UX Design Intern for the Healthcare Department on the Oral Care Team. Last summer, I was working for Samsung as a Visual Design Intern for the Mobile Application team creating/iterating a super cool application that will be launched in 2018!


I am passionate about creating innovative designs that tackle complex problems. I find a certain fascination with the idea of developing worthwhile experiences for individuals. To me, the design process is enjoyable due to its intertwined relationship between the experiences people have with art. I treasure helping others discover depth in meaningful prototypes. I want to impact the world one step at a time through valuable ideas!
